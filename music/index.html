High Hoax : Valiant Effort

Recorded in my living room, mixed and mastered in Pro Tools on a shoe-string budget. "Valiant Effort" is the EP for our project, D.I.Y.ing to exist.

Singer/ Songwriter Austin Baker, along with drummer and songwriter Jake Stringer, and bass player Sam Walker, wrote the album together over the course of the project. Baker plays with concepts about life and death, the dangers of hedonism, and finding value in your relationship with others.

Memories

My first EP, which explores a collection of compositions that I have written in my lifetime. I recorded everything in my humble home studio. Special thanks to my brother, Ian Derrickson for his help arranging percussion: to my dad for playing drums on "Where are you now?", and to Jake Stringer for playing drums on "Confession".

The Bike Hero Soundtrack

The Bike Hero Soundtrack is a collection of music that I composed for the short film, Bike Hero. All music was written, recorded and composed in my home studio.

Warrior Hunter
