Bike Hero: A Rider is Born

Bike Hero was a project that I produced with my friend, and trials bike rider, Ryan Bratton, as well as my cousin, Timm Derrickson. The goal was to create a fun promotional video that demonstrated Ryan's riding ability. In the process, we thought it would be funner to write a comedic action hero storyline to show off his "super human" bike skills! We filmed the entire short video over the course of 1 weekend on location in Bentonville, Arkansas with:

  • 1 DSLR - Nikon D5300
  • 3 Go Pro's
  • Our Cell Phones
  • Rode Video Mic
  • A Tascam Field Recorder
  • 2 shure Pencil Mics

High Hoax : D.I.Y.ing to Exist

High Hoax was my senior thesis in college as a part of my Electronic Arts: Audio Studies degree. The short film documents the creation, marketing, and success of building a band from scratch. In this position, I wore many hats. Not only was I the audio production lead for both sound for video and recording the High Hoax EP, I was a videographer, graphic designer, promotor, set designer.

The Extraordinary Tale of Katie Bauer's Breakup

As an elective class, I did an independent study in which I lead the sound design team for student short film "The Extraordinary Tale of Katie Bauer's Breakup." Filming took place over 3 months in Springfield, Missouri.
