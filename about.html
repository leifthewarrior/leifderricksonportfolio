About Leif Derrickson

Thank you so much for visiting my portfolio! My name is Leif Derrickson. People often call me a jack-of-all-trades and would say that I am very passionate with my work. I love learning new things and working with my hands. I have interests in both music and art, surrounding myself with people who have similar interest in order to learn from them and offer my skills as well. I am most passionate about writing music. Music has always been a form of escapism for me. When I work, my mind is filled with song, helping to set a rhythm for my work flow and to fuel my imagination. I am happiest when I am able to create. I love the process of discovry and idea generation, then taking it through production to distribution.

I hope that you enjoy my work and I invite you to check back from time to time to see any new proje that I may be working on!
